About us

ICEPICK is your firendly neighbourhood locksmith, founded in 2022 by a disgrunteled engineering drop out. We mix icecold professionalism with cool a demeanor. With three employees and a small shop in the boonies we work harder than anyone else to make sure you stay safe and protected.
